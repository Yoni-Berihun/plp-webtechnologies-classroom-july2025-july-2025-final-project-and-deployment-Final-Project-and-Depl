The Birthplace of Coffee

Ethiopia is the birthplace of coffee, and Hawassa is at the heart of this rich tradition. Our coffee beans are sourced directly from local farmers in the Sidamo and Yirgacheffe regions, ensuring the highest quality and supporting our community.

We honor the traditional Ethiopian coffee ceremony, where coffee is roasted, ground, and brewed in front of you using our authentic jebena (coffee pot). This ritual has been passed down through generations and represents the social heart of Ethiopian culture.

Why Ethiopian Coffee is Special

  • Grown at high altitudes (1,500-2,200 meters)
  • Natural processing methods preserve unique flavors
  • Diverse microclimates create distinct regional profiles
  • Direct trade relationships with local farmers
