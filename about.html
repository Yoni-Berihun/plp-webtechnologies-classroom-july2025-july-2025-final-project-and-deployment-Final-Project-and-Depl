About YO-coffee

Founded in 2020 in the heart of Hawassa, YO-coffee celebrates Ethiopia's rich coffee heritage. We are passionate about sharing the authentic Ethiopian coffee experience while supporting local farmers and preserving traditional brewing methods.

Our Coffee Heritage

Ethiopia is the birthplace of coffee, and we honor this legacy by sourcing our beans directly from local farmers in the Sidamo, Yirgacheffe, and Harrar regions. Our coffee ceremony is a time-honored tradition that brings people together and celebrates the social aspect of coffee culture.

Our Coffee Regions

Sidamo

Known for its bright acidity and citrus notes, Sidamo coffee is grown at high altitudes and processed using traditional methods.

Yirgacheffe

Famous for its floral and tea-like characteristics, Yirgacheffe coffee is considered one of the world's finest single-origin coffees.

Harrar

Dry-processed Harrar coffee offers bold, wine-like flavors with a full body and distinctive blueberry notes.
